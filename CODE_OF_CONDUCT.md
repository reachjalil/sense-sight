# Code of Conduct

SenseSight is an open-source robotics project. Contributors are expected to
work with technical rigor and respect.

## Expected Behavior

- Be precise, constructive, and professional.
- Assume good intent while still making problems concrete.
- Keep discussions focused on the work, evidence, and tradeoffs.
- Respect different experience levels across robotics, web, infrastructure, and
  design.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions after maintainers redirect the
  thread.
- Any conduct that makes the project unsafe or unwelcoming.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; block accounts; or
take other action needed to protect the project. Report concerns privately to
the maintainers through GitHub.
