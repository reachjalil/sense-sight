# Product

SenseSight is a pivot from Human Sense toward the world-generation layer.

The product promise:

> See what the robot sees. Understand what it knows. Decide what it should do.

The center of gravity is now realtime spatial memory:

- robot-eye RGB and depth context
- LiDAR, pose, odometry, IMU, and robot-state synchronization
- live point-cloud previews
- Gaussian-splat-ready reconstruction assets
- human-in-the-loop correction and trust boundaries

The reverse singularity thesis remains: use human judgment to improve robotic
spatial intelligence instead of pretending full autonomy resolves every edge
case.
